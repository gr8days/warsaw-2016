Technipelago AB is a Swedish company that develops custom web and mobile applications for small and medium sized businesses.

Grails and it's plugin architecture has been a major success factor for Technipelago. With a large toolbox of Grails plugins and cloud deployment we deliver custom applications in a cost effective way and with short time to production.

Many of our Grails plugins are available as open source under the name GR8 CRM (gr8crm.github.io).
