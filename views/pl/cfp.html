Call for Papers

The GR8Day Warsaw Call For Papers is open! Submit your talks here. The CFP site is shared with GR8Conf Denmark and US to save you time when you want to submit your talks to all three of the conferences.

Submit your talks!

General topic guidelines

The conference covers technologies in the Groovy ecosystem, such as Grails, Griffon, Spock, GPars, Gradle and many others. Talks should be technical enough that they include code (although not a requirement).

The standard time slot for a presentation at GR8Day ranges from 15 minutes to 90 minutes, with a main focus on 50 minutes talks. University sessions are three to four hours, including time for one 15 minute snack break.

We encourage technical talks, supporting material, and references to provide conference attendees with additional details.

Conference topics

When you submit your talk, please tag your presentation with one or more of these tags, and add your own, if one is missing:

  • Groovy
  • Grails
  • Griffon
  • Spring Boot
  • Building
  • Testing
  • Parallelization
  • Meta programming
  • Frontend
  • Backend
  • HTML5 / CSS3 / Javascript
  • Cloud
  • NoSQL
  • Mobile
  • In production / Use cases
  • Security
  • Integration

We ask, that a copy of the slidedeck is made available to the crew after the presentation, so that we can publish it online (Slideshare or alike)

All presentations are subject to being recorded and made available to the public without further remuneration to the presenter. Do not include any proprietary material that cannot meet this requirement.
